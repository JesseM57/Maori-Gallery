This is a Maori Meeting House in South Island. Each of these has its unique history and significance. Some examples are Otakou Maori Memorial Methodist Church, Te Rau Aroha Marae and Te Whatu Manawa Maoritanga O Rehua
This image shows the All Blacks doing the Haka at a Rugby World Cup. A Haka is a traditional New Zelanad performance, often performend at sports tournaments or at schools.
Maori Kai is a traditional way of sharing food with others. Kai is often shared at events such as Marariki.
Maori weaving is a traditional way of sewing to make things such as baskets.
